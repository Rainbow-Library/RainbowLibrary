# Rainbow Library Website and assets #
The Rainbow Library is a welcoming community space in VRChat created for transgender people and their allies. It offers a safe, supportive environment where people can connect, socialize, and be themselves without fear of judgment. Whether someone is looking for friendship, advice, or simply a place to relax, the Rainbow Library encourages kindness, respect, and inclusivity.

The community hosts conversations, social events, and opportunities for members to share their experiences and support one another. For many people, it serves as a place to build confidence, explore their identity, and meet others who understand similar journeys. While the space is centered around supporting transgender individuals, respectful allies are also welcome to help foster a positive and accepting atmosphere.

At its heart, the Rainbow Library is about creating a sense of belonging. It reminds people that they are not alone and provides a virtual home where everyone is encouraged to express themselves authentically, make meaningful connections, and feel accepted for who they are.
